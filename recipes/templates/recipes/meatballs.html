Meatballs are savory, bite-sized balls made from ground meat mixed with seasonings and binders like breadcrumbs or eggs. They are often served with pasta, on sandwiches, or as appetizers. Meatballs are a staple in Italian cuisine and are also popular in many other cultures around the world, including Swedish, Turkish, and Chinese cuisines. Meatballs can be a good source of protein and essential nutrients like iron and B vitamins, depending on the type of meat used and the cooking method.

Instructions

Step 1

Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. Roll mixture into small balls.

Step 2

Bake in the oven or cook in a skillet until browned and cooked through.

Ingredients

Ground meat (beef, pork, or a mix), breadcrumbs, egg, minced onion, garlic powder, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning.
