Tsire (Suya)

Tsire Suya is a popular Nigerian street food consisting of skewered and grilled meat (usually beef or chicken) seasoned with a spicy peanut marinade and served with sliced onions and spicy pepper sauce. Suya is a beloved snack in Nigeria and is often enjoyed as a quick bite or as part of a social gathering. Tsire Suya provides protein from the meat and healthy fats from the peanut marinade. It can be a flavorful and satisfying snack or meal option.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Tags : Appetizer (5) Beverage (3) Desert (3) Main-course (3) Salad (3) Soup & Stew (3)

Instructions

Step 1

Cut meat into thin strips and thread onto skewers.

Step 2

Mix peanut butter with ground ginger, ground garlic, cayenne pepper, and salt to make a marinade.

Step 3

Coat meat skewers with marinade and let sit for at least an hour.

Step 4

Grill skewers until meat is cooked through and slightly charred.

Ingredients

Beef or chicken, peanut butter, ground ginger, ground garlic, cayenne pepper, salt, skewers.
