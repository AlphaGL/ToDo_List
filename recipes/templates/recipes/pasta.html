Pasta Pesto Farfalle is a delicious Italian dish featuring farfalle pasta (bowtie pasta) tossed in a vibrant basil pesto sauce, typically made with fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. Pesto originated in Genoa, Italy, and is a popular sauce used in Italian cuisine, often paired with various types of pasta. This dish offers a good balance of carbohydrates, healthy fats from olive oil and pine nuts, and some protein from the Parmesan cheese. It can be made even healthier by using whole wheat pasta.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Tags : Appetizer (5) Beverage (3) Desert (3) Main-course (3) Salad (3) Soup & Stew (3)

Instructions

Step 1

Cook farfalle pasta according to package instructions.

Step 2

Drain pasta and toss with basil pesto sauce until evenly coated.

Step 3

Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese and pine nuts if desired.

Ingredients

Farfalle pasta, basil pesto sauce, grated Parmesan cheese, pine nuts (optional).
