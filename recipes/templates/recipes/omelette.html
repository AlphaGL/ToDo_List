Omelette

An omelette is a classic dish made by whisking eggs with milk and cooking them in a skillet until set. It can be filled with various ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or meats. Omelettes have been enjoyed for centuries and are a versatile dish found in cuisines around the world, from French omelettes to Spanish tortillas. Omelettes are a good source of protein and can be packed with nutrients depending on the fillings used. They can be made healthier by incorporating vegetables and using less oil or cheese.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Tags :Appetizer (5) Beverage (3) Desert (3) Main-course (3) Salad (3) Soup & Stew (3)

Instructions

Step 1

Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.

Step 2

Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat.

Step 3

Pour in egg mixture. As eggs start to set, add fillings to one half of the omelette.

Step 4

Fold the other half over the fillings. Cook until eggs are fully set.

Ingredients

Eggs, milk, salt, pepper, butter or oil, fillings (such as cheese, vegetables, ham).
