Cake is a sweet baked dessert made from flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and other ingredients. It comes in various flavors and forms, from simple sponge cakes to elaborate layer cakes. Cakes have been part of celebrations and special occasions for centuries, with different cultures having their own traditional cake recipes and customs. Cakes are indulgent treats high in sugar and calories. While they provide energy, they should be enjoyed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

Instructions

Step 1

Cream butter and sugar together. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.

Step 2

Mix in flour and baking powder alternately with milk until batter is smooth.

Step 3

Pour into greased cake pans and bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Ingredients

Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, vanilla extract, milk.
