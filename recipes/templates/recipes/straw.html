Strawberry salad

Strawberry salad is a refreshing dish made with fresh strawberries, mixed greens, crumbled cheese (such as feta or goat cheese), nuts, and a tangy balsamic vinaigrette dressing. Strawberries are not only delicious but also packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, making them a nutritious addition to salads. This salad is low in calories and rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants from the strawberries and greens. Nuts provide healthy fats and protein, while the cheese adds calcium and flavor.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

Tags : Appetizer (5) Beverage (3) Desert (3) Main-course (3) Salad (3) Soup & Stew (3)

Instructions

Step 1

Wash and slice strawberries. Toss with mixed greens, crumbled feta cheese, and nuts.

Step 2

Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing before serving.

Ingredients

Fresh strawberries, mixed greens, feta cheese, balsamic vinaigrette dressing, nuts (such as walnuts or pecans).
