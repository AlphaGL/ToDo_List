Cocoa coffee is a delicious beverage made by adding cocoa powder to hot brewed coffee. It offers a rich and indulgent flavor with a hint of chocolate. Cocoa coffee is often enjoyed as a cozy treat during cold weather or as a dessert beverage. It can also be customized with additions like whipped cream or marshmallows. Cocoa coffee provides antioxidants from cocoa powder and a moderate amount of caffeine from coffee, along with potential health benefits associated with both ingredients when consumed in moderation.

prep time

8 min.

Tags : Appetizer (5) Beverage (3) Desert (3) Main-course (3) Salad (3) Soup & Stew (3)

Instructions

Step 1

Stir cocoa powder into hot brewed coffee until dissolved.

Step 2

Add sugar to taste if desired. Stir in milk or cream for added creaminess.

Ingredients

Hot brewed coffee, cocoa powder, sugar (optional), milk or cream (optional).
